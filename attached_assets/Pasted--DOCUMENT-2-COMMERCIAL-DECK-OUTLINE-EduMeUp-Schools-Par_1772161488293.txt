SECTION 8: RISK ANALYSIS & MITIGATION (5 pages)
Potential Risks and Mitigation Strategies:
RISK 1: Low Student Adoption
Risk Description:
Only 40-50% students adopt platform (below 70% target)
Results in lower revenue than projected
Mitigation:
Strong school endorsement (principal communication)
Parent information sessions (show value)
Early success stories (pilot class results)
Student testimonials (peer influence)
Free trial period (reduce barrier)
Fallback:
Model 3 (Integrated Tuition) guarantees 100% adoption
Charter discount offsets lower adoption revenue

RISK 2: Teacher Resistance
Risk Description:
Teachers refuse to use platform
Implementation fails despite investment
Mitigation:
Comprehensive training (20-25 hours)
Show immediate time savings (5-6 hours/week)
Start with willing teachers (pilot)
Peer influence (early adopters convince others)
Leadership mandate (expectation set)
Historical Data:
20% enthusiastic immediately
60% willing with support
20% resistant initially → 90% satisfied by Month 3

RISK 3: Technical Infrastructure Issues
Risk Description:
Internet connectivity problems
Device availability insufficient
Mitigation:
Pre-implementation infrastructure assessment
Cloud-based platform (low bandwidth requirements)
Offline access options (download content)
School computer lab access (backup)
Mobile-responsive design (works on phones)

Teacher time saved × hourly rate

Calculated value based on salary data

Additional teacher hiring avoided
External consulting avoided
Investment Required 
Time investment (administrators, teachers)
Valued at average hourly rates
Upfront cash: $0
ROI Formula:
ROI = (Total Benefits - Total Investment) / Total Investment × 100
